STEP 1
THE PROBLEM

We All Fall Short

"for all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God."

— Romans 3:23 (NIV)

Sin isn't just "big" mistakes. It's anything that falls short of God's perfect holiness — our thoughts, words, and actions.

This separation from God is the root of the brokenness we see in the world and feel in our own hearts.

STEP 2
THE CONSEQUENCE

The Bad News & The Gift

"For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord."

— Romans 6:23 (NIV)

The "wages" (what we earn) for sin is death — not just physical death, but eternal separation from God.

But right in the middle of the bad news comes the greatest gift: eternal life offered freely through Jesus.

STEP 3
GOD'S LOVE

While We Were Still Sinners

"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."

— Romans 5:8 (NIV)

God didn't wait for us to clean up our lives first. He sent Jesus to die for us while we were still in rebellion against Him.

This is love of the highest order — sacrificial, undeserved, and powerful enough to bridge the gap sin created.

STEP 4
YOUR RESPONSE

Believe & Be Saved

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved."

— Romans 10:9 (NIV)

Salvation isn't earned by good works or religious effort. It is received by faith — trusting completely in Jesus' finished work on the cross and His resurrection.

It begins with a heart change and a simple, sincere declaration.

YOUR NEXT STEP

A Simple Prayer

"Lord Jesus, I know I have sinned and need Your forgiveness. I believe You died for my sins and rose again. I turn from my old life and trust You as my Lord and Savior. Come into my heart and change me. Thank You for the gift of eternal life. In Jesus' name, Amen."

Praise God! Decision recorded.
